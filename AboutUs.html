Our favorite Austivities:

Thinkery

5/5 | All Ages

The Thinkery museum offers families a hands-on learning environment where you can learn more about the science behind many ideas through different experiences.

Closed Monday/Tuesday

https://thinkeryaustin.org/

1830 Simond Ave, Austin, Texas 78723

Blanton Museum of Art

5/5 | All Ages

The Blanton Museum of Art has more than 19,000 works of art and is one of the largest university art museums in the United States.

Closed Monday/Tuesday

https://blantonmuseum.org/

200 E Martin Luther King Jr Blvd, Austin, TX 78712

Austin Central Library

5/5 | Free | All Ages

The Austin Central Library is a public library with many branches that is run by the City of Austin. They offer a quiet place to read/study as well as many places to rent for events.

Closed Sunday

https://library.austintexas.gov/central-library

710 W Cesar Chavez St, Austin, TX 78701

Bullock Texas State History Museum

5/5 | All Ages

The Bullock Texas State History Museum is a history museum that focuses on the "Story of Texas" in an educational and interactive way.

Closed Monday/Tuesday

https://www.thestoryoftexas.com/

1800 Congress Ave, Austin, TX 78701
